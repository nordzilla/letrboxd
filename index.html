LetrBoxd

A solver for the Letter Boxed puzzle game that finds solutions without repeated letters.

The LetrBoxd solver uses the open-source Wordnik word list, which may produce solutions that are not accepted by the official Letter Boxed game.

Use the mouse or arrow keys to navigate within the puzzle input. Update the puzzle input by focusing a letter and replacing it. Expand the solution lists to view solutions.
